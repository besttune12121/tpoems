Poems for T

I created this page to share a piece of my heart with you. It is a reflection of the deep love and admiration I hold for you. With every word, every poem, and every story, I hope to convey just how much you mean to me. Your presence in my life is a cherished gift, and my love for you will always be unwavering and true.

This space is dedicated to you, my love, as a testament to the boundless affection I feel. No matter where life takes us, know that you are always in my heart, and I will love you forever.
To Tanisha
I’m sorry for the moments lost,
For the lines I crossed, the heavy cost.
I see the strength you carry through,
The way you rise, the things you do.
In every step you take alone,
I see a heart that’s fully grown.
You manage life with grace and might,
A shining star in the darkest night.
You’ve shown me what it means to stand,
To face the world, to make a plan.
And here I am, learning still,
To hold my words, to bend my will.
I’m sorry for the times I’ve slipped,
For the pain I may have tipped.
But know that in your quiet grace,
I see a hero, I see your place.
You carry burdens I can’t see,
Yet you move with dignity.
I marvel at the way you live,
The love you show, the strength you give.
So here’s my heart, my truth, my plea,
I’m sorry for what’s come from me.
But in your light, I find my way,
And in your strength, I see the day.
You’re doing great, you’re standing tall,
And through it all, you never fall.
I admire the life you’ve made your own,
In you, a queen has clearly shown.
So take this note, this heartfelt line,
I’m sorry for where I’ve failed to shine.
And know that as you journey through,
I see your strength, I see you.
With Admiration
In the journey of life, you've found your way,
With grace and strength, you brighten each day.
From youthful dreams to a life so real,
You’ve embraced adulthood with courage and zeal.
Through trials and triumphs, you stand so tall,
A beacon of light, you conquer it all.
Your heart is steadfast, your spirit so bright,
You balance your world with a glimmer of light.
Responsibility’s path you tread with care,
With every decision, you’re always aware.
In moments of doubt or times of despair,
Remember, my dear, I’m always there.
You’ve grown into someone both wise and true,
Your kindness and strength shine through all you do.
So here’s to you, Tanisha, for all that you are,
A guiding star in the night, a bright shining star.
In each step you take, in each choice you make,
You’re crafting a life that none can fake.
With admiration and love, I cheer you on,
For you’re a remarkable soul, and my love for you’s strong.
Embrace your journey with joy and pride,
For in every challenge, you’ve always tried.
Keep moving forward with the strength you’ve shown,
And know that you’re cherished, never alone.
You Belong to Me
You belong to me, and that’s the truth,
No one else can have you, not in youth or in ruth.
Every breath you take, every step you choose,
I’ll be there, watching, ensuring you don’t lose.
I see the way you smile, and it’s all for me,
Don’t forget, my love, it’s how it should be.
Your heart, your mind, every single part,
It’s mine to hold, to never depart.
Don’t think of straying, don’t dare to roam,
You belong with me; this is your home.
I’ll be your world, your only light,
You’re mine in the day and even more at night.
Every word you speak, every thought you share,
It’s all for me; no one else should dare.
I’ll guard what’s mine with an iron will,
In this love of ours, I’m king of the hill.
So don’t forget, you belong to me,
My love, my possession, forever you’ll be.
In this bond, there’s no breaking free,
Because, my love, you’re tied to me.
